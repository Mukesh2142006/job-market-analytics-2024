\# Job Market Analytics Dashboard



A complete end-to-end data analytics portfolio project analyzing 12,000+ 

real-world job postings using Python, SQL, and Power BI.



\---



\## Project Overview



This project analyzes job market trends for data roles to help aspiring 

Data Analysts understand which skills to learn, which cities have the most 

opportunities, and what experience level is most commonly required.



\---



\## Key Questions Answered



\- Which skills are most demanded in data jobs?

\- Which cities have the most job postings?

\- Which companies hire the most data professionals?

\- What experience level is required most often?

\- How are jobs distributed across countries?

\- What is the breakdown of remote vs onsite jobs?



\---



\## Key Findings



\- Machine Learning is the #1 most demanded skill (803 job postings)

\- Senior Data Engineer is the most posted role (288 postings)

\- New York, NY leads with 280 job postings

\- 78.5% of jobs are based in the United States

\- 89% of roles require Mid-Senior experience level

\- 99.8% of jobs are Onsite — remote work is still rare in data roles
